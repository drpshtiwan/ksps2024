About Us

Welcome to the 3rd International Aesthetic & Reconstructive Conference (IARC) hosted by the Kurdistan Society of Plastic Surgeons and organized by Krossmate Company. Taking place on May 30th-31st, 2024, at the prestigious Erbil-Rotana Hotel, this conference serves as a global platform for plastic surgeon specialists and trainees from around the world to convene and exchange insights, innovations, and advancements in the field of plastic surgery. Supported by the Ministry of Health (MOH) and the Doctor’s Syndicate of KRG and Federal Iraq, IARC aims to foster innovation, exchange ideas, and push the boundaries of plastic surgery on a global scale.
